Privacy Policy

CVNova AI stores the account information and career content needed to provide resume building, AI writing, analysis, matching, and export features.

Resume content may include personal details, work history, education, skills, links, uploaded document text, generated cover letters, LinkedIn content, and interview preparation notes.

Passwords are hashed, forms are protected with CSRF validation, and application secrets are managed through environment variables. API keys entered in local development are stored in the local ignored `.env` file; production keys should be configured in the hosting provider environment.

When live AI is enabled, relevant resume or job-description content is sent to the selected provider, OpenAI or Gemini, to generate the requested output. Do not submit information you do not want processed by that provider.

CVNova AI does not sell user resume content. Users should review generated content before using it in job applications.
